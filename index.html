About Me

Howdy. I'm an astronomy graduate student at Georgia State University. I am interested in low-mass stars and their application to Galactic Archaeology. I am involved in the fifth Sloan Digital Sky Survey (SDSS-V) as a Solar Neighborhood Census working group chair and as a Database Manager.
